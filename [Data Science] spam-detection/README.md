## Project Objectives
Based on an email dataset provided by the CTO, the project aims to:

- Train a **classifier** to identify SPAM emails.
- Identify the **main topics** among emails classified as SPAM.
- Compute the **semantic distance between topics** to evaluate the heterogeneity of SPAM content.
- Extract information about **organizations mentioned** in **NON-SPAM** emails.

---

## Added Value
Email analysis provides ProfessionAI with several strategic advantages:

### Improved Anti-Spam Filtering
An efficient classifier significantly reduces the number of unwanted emails reaching inboxes, optimizing the management of corporate communications.

### In-Depth Content Analysis
Identifying the main topics within SPAM emails makes it possible to uncover trends, recurring themes, and patterns, strengthening cybersecurity strategies.

### Heterogeneity Assessment
Measuring semantic distance between topics helps assess the diversity of SPAM content, enabling better defenses against a wide range of attack types.

### Organization Identification
Extracting organizations from legitimate emails can be leveraged to enhance **business intelligence** processes and improve communication management with clients and partners.
